Our Speciality

It took us years of practice, baking for our friends’ and families’ birthdays to attain the level of skills to finally take in orders! We’ve kept our practice to treat each and every of our customers as our family and we put in love and passion in every cake we bake.

HandMade Freshly with Love

Irresistable Handmade treats. Cakes should be delicious and a guilt-free pleasure. We strive to deliver wholesome desserts low in sugar and cream whilst not implicating the taste of our baked goods. All our bakes have no added preservatives added.

Themed and Customised to your Liking

Have a specific design of cupcake you want? No problem! Send us an image of what you want on your cake, and we will make it happen for you! We offer them in bite-sized editions so that you can get to try a plethora of flavours and goodies without going overboard!

Round the clock Delivery

In this era and age we live in, convenience is key. Hence, we offer prompt island-wide delivery service. Hassle free midnight surprises made possible. We deliver via hourly time slots 24/7, and we are always on time.
